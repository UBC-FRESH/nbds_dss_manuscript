\opening{Dear Editors-in-Chief,}

We are pleased to submit our manuscript entitled \textit{“Developing a decision support system to assess forest management scenarios as a nature-based decarbonization solution: A case study from a mining company in British Columbia, Canada”} for consideration for publication in \textit{Cleaner Environmental Systems}. 

While the mining sector is under pressure to meet net-zero emissions by 2050, it is likely to fall short of this target due to technological and financial barriers. Hence, it is important to explore alternative approaches such as nature-based solutions to address emission reduction. This study investigates the role of nature-based solutions in supporting the decarbonization efforts within the mining sector. We introduce an open source analytical decision support system to evaluate how alternative forest management strategies can contribute to these goals. The research also provides an assessment of the economic, environmental, and social impacts associated with different forest management scenarios.

We believe that this manuscript aligns well with the objectives and scope of \textit{Cleaner Environmental Systems} and will be of interest to readers concerned with sustainable industrial
practices, land use planning, and climate change mitigation.

We confirm that this manuscript has not been published previously and is not being considered elsewhere. All authors have reviewed and approved the final version of the manuscript and consent to its submission. The manuscript, title page, and \textit{Declaration of competing interest} statement have been uploaded through the journal’s online submission system.

Thank you for considering our manuscript. We look forward to your response.
